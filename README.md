## About the Game

**Fields of Red** is a short first person horror experience built around tension, restraint, and awareness.

You cross quiet fields under the constant feeling that something is above you, watching.  
Your lantern is not just a tool. It whispers, warns, and becomes your only guide through the dark.

The game was created around the theme **You Are Not Alone**, with a focus on slow burn fear rather than constant action.

---

## Core Gameplay

- Use your lantern wisely
- Turn off the light when danger is near
- Hide in bushes to break line of sight
- Move carefully through the fields
- Listen to the whispers and react in time
